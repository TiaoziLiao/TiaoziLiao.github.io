## About Me

Chao Liao is currently a Full-Time Researcher at ByteDance Seed. He received his B.S. from Shenzhen University (SZU) and his M.S. from Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT). He previously worked as a Research Intern at Peng Cheng Laboratory (PCL) and as a Full-Time Researcher at Kuaishou Technology.

## Research Interests

His current research focuses on **unified models for multimodal understanding and generation**. His previous research covers **model compression**, **neural architecture search (NAS)**, and **multimodal large language models (MLLMs)**.
